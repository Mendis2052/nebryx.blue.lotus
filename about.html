Our Foundation

Nebryx Blue Lotus LTD was founded on a simple principle: to offer an uncompromised standard of excellence across multiple sectors of the automotive trade. We span across premium light motor vehicles, reliable commercial fleets, and an extensive catalog of wholesale and retail auto parts.

Operating under multi-faceted SIC classifications (45112, 45190, 45310, 45320), we possess the unique ability to cater to individual car enthusiasts, growing small businesses, and large-scale industrial garages—all under one unified, premium brand.
